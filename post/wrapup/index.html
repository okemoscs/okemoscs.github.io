End of the year

Jun 5, 2017 · 1 minute read
Update

The year is now drawing to a close. This year, Okemos CS Club went to Spartahack, where we competed, and many of our members learned basic programming skills. Next year, we will be going to both MHacks, Spartahack, and we will be competiting in the governor’s cyber security challenge. I reccomend learning some of the basics of cybersecurity if you wish to participate.

Next year, our meetings will be every Tuesday, from 2:45 to 3:45. See you then! Good luck with finals!
