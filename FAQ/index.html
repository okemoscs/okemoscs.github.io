FAQ

When do we meet?

We meet on Tuesdays in Mr. Harrod’s room (Room No) from 3:00 - 3:45

When is the first meeting?

Our first meeting is on Oct. 11th, for the 2016-2017 school year. This meeting will probably be about 30 minutes to give new members a good idea of what this club is about.

Do I need to bring anything?

You should bring in your student or personal device to code on, and some scrap paper to sketch out ideas. Even if you aren’t a designer, scrap paper can be useful for logic diagrams before you start coding.
